    What is an open initiative?

    Any organization, initiative, or startup that follows these core principles:

    Open

    We do our work in the open. Learning, reusing, and building upon our work is encouraged.

    Collaborative

    We work together with the communities we serve. Everyone is invited to get involved.

    Impact-Driven

    We focus on creating lasting positive impact for people and planet.
